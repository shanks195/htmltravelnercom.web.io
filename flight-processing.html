Flight Booking Process

Book flight tickets with only 4 steps

Step 1:

Search Flights

Searching your suitable flights by choosing type of your destination , your trip (one way, round trip or multi cities), arrival date, return date, number of travellers, class,... to find the best deals for you.

Search Flights

Step 2:

Fill your information

You need to fill out the online form for all passengers by providing us full name, gender, date of birth, passport details that match the passenger's passport and contact details.

Step 3:

Make the payment

You can pay by Credit / Debit card, Paypal Account, or wire transfer to our HSBC Bank account. After the payment, you will receive an email asking for other requested information to finish your application.

Step 4:

Receive the ticket

After you make payment successful to complete for issue E-Ticket. We will issue E-Ticket for you and send to you via email. Print off your ticket and take it with you when come to airport.

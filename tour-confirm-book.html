About Best of Vietnam 15 days

See the very best of Vietnam on a highlights tour designed to give you just enough time to savour your travel experience at a leisurely pace. Starting with the majestic beauty in the North such as spectacular scenery in Mai Chau, the cave in Halong Bay, discover the country’s bustling capital and its historic monuments before heading South for a experience rural life by boat trip in Mekong Delta. This tour will definitely offer you a fascinating experience about Vietnamese Culture as well as Vietnamese gastronomy

Highlights

Explore an array of city attractions in Hanoi and hidden lanes and alleys in the Old Quarter.

An overnight cruise in Halong Bay in a wooden junk with visits to one of its amazing caves

Discover the best top things to do in Hue including Imperial Enclosure, The Emperor King's tomb and river cruise along Perfume River with a visit to Thien Mu Pagoda.

A strolling or leisure biking tour to discover Hoi An ancient town and see the best of Hoi An has to offer

Discover the breath of life in Mekong Delta and savor every moment among the lushly green vegetation and mighty waterways, immersed in the rustic living of riverside province.
